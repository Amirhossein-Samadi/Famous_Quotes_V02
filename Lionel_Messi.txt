Lionel Messi has said many inspirational quotes about hard work, dedication, and the importance of enjoying the game. 
Hard work
"I start early and I stay late, day after day, year after year, it took me 17 years and 114 days to become an overnight success". 
"I always knew that to do that I'd have to make a lot of sacrifices". 
"You have to fight to reach your dream. You have to sacrifice and work hard for it". 
Enjoyment 
"I have fun like a child in the street".
"What I do is play soccer, which is what I like".
Teamwork 
"I'm lucky to be part of a team who help to make me look good".
"I prefer to win titles with the team ahead of individual awards or scoring more goals than anyone else".
